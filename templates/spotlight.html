BTS, the South Korean boy-band, has become the world's biggest boy band, captivating millions of fans worldwide. The group's seven members, including RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, have overcome great obstacles to achieve their success, starting with nothing but each other and a shared love for music. They have shattered stereotypes, clich√©s, and records along the way.

However, their success has not come without sacrifices. The group has worked incredibly hard to reach their level of fame, spending countless sleepless nights and enduring a rocky start. Despite their uncertain beginnings, they have persevered thanks to their passion and the support of their fans, known as ARMY.

In the highly competitive k-pop industry, where people are often expected to work like machines, BTS has managed to maintain their humanity and use their platform to address social issues and positively impact millions of ARMYs' lives.

Their musical genius and captivating performances have earned them the admiration of many artists. They have collaborated with Nicki Minaj, Charlie XCX, Steve Aoki, Lauv, Zara Larsson, Halsey, and many others.

BTS's impressive list of accomplishments includes numerous awards such as the Golden Disk Award in 2022, 2021, and 2020; American Music Award in 2021; Seoul Music Award in 2023, 2022, 2021, and 2020; MAMA Artist Award of the Year in 2022, 2021, and 2020; Billboard Music Award for Top Duo/Group in 2019, 2020, and 2021; People's Choice Award in 2022, among many others.

However, the group's cohesion has been disrupted as two members have enlisted in compulsory military service in Korea. While the other five members continue to produce music, the group remains incomplete without all of its members. ARMYs eagerly await BTS's return as a complete band after their enlistment.
