Welcome to Euterpe, a music-focused website created by Keerthi, a dedicated ECE student at IIIT-H. My goal is to provide a platform that showcases the top artists and their most popular songs based on reviews, creating an IMDb equivalent for music enthusiasts.

I have put a significant amount of effort into designing and developing this website. My passion for music and commitment to creating an exceptional user experience has driven me to build a site that not only looks beautiful but also provides valuable insights into the music industry.

Allow me to introduce myself. Keerthi Seela, an 18-year-old embodiment of determination and genuine kindness, navigates through challenges with grace and enthusiasm, motivating those around her to embrace a more upbeat perspective on life.

I hope you enjoy exploring Euterpe and discovering new music. As I continue to develop this project, I will update my About page with new information, so stay tuned!
